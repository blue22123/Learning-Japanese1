  • 台所 (だいどころ - daidokoro) - English: Kitchen - Indonesian: Dapur
  • バスルーム (ばするーむ - basurūmu) - English: Bathroom - Indonesian: Kamar mandi
  • リビング (りびんぐ - ribingu) - English: Living room - Indonesian: Ruang tamu
  • 庭 (にわ - niwa) - English: Garden - Indonesian: Taman
  • 窓 (まど - mado) - English: Window - Indonesian: Jendela
  • ドア (どあ - doa) - English: Door - Indonesian: Pintu
  • 屋根 (やね - yane) - English: Roof - Indonesian: Atap
  • 階段 (かいだん - kaidan) - English: Stairs - Indonesian: Tangga
  • 家具 (かぐ - kagu) - English: Furniture - Indonesian: Perabotan

Greetings (挨拶 - Aisatsu)

Click to learn common Japanese greetings

Common Greetings:

  • おはようございます (Ohayou gozaimasu) - English: Good morning - Indonesian: Selamat pagi
  • こんにちは (Konnichiwa) - English: Hello/Good afternoon - Indonesian: Halo/Selamat siang/sore
  • こんばんは (Konbanwa) - English: Good evening - Indonesian: Selamat malam
  • おやすみなさい (Oyasuminasai) - English: Good night - Indonesian: Selamat malam
  • さようなら (Sayounara) - English: Goodbye - Indonesian: Selamat tinggal
  • ありがとう (Arigatou) - English: Thank you - Indonesian: Terima kasih
  • すみません (Sumimasen) - English: Excuse me/I'm sorry - Indonesian: Permisi/Aku minta maaf
  • お元気ですか (Ogenki desu ka) - English: How are you? - Indonesian: Apa kabar?
  • ただいま (Tadaima) - English: I'm home (used when returning home) - Indonesian: Saya sudah di rumah (digunakan saat kembali ke rumah)
  • はじめまして (Hajimemashite) - English: Nice to meet you - Indonesian: Senang bertemu dengan Anda
  • いってきます (Ittekimasu) - English: I'm off (used when leaving home) - Indonesian: Saya pergi (digunakan saat meninggalkan rumah)
  • お久しぶりです (Ohisashiburi desu) - English: Long time no see - Indonesian: Sudah lama tidak bertemu
  • お疲れ様です (Otsukaresama desu) - English: Thank you for your hard work - Indonesian: Terima kasih atas kerja keras Anda
  • いらっしゃいませ (Irasshaimase) - English: Welcome (used in shops/restaurants) - Indonesian: Selamat datang (digunakan di toko/restoran)
